The presently wolf-sized red fox leaped onto Harry's bed, sitting down at the end and shrinking down into the size of a normal red fox. He flicked his black tipped ears at Harry. : Why must you yell so often? The red fox said through telepathy, his ears laid flat against his head. Pain fired through Sam's entire body, and he suddenly felt weak and his legs gave out from under him. He felt the noose around his neck and suddenly, he was being lifted. Sam, whose eyes had been squinted shut from the pain, managed to look down and catch a glimpse of what the man below him was doing. Sam closed his eyes again, listening to the whistle of the knife flying through the air, more than likely aimed for his head. And then everything went silent. Pain disappeared.
